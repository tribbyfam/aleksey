About Aleksey

Born in 1957 Vilnus, Lithuania to a military family Aleksey started playing hockey at age 7 in Riga, Latvia. He played for the Dinamo Riga organization in Latvia from peewee to Elite level.

In 1980 at the age of 23, Aleksey moved back to Vilnus, Lithuania where he started a coaching job in the small town of Elektrenai, with a population of 14,000 and only 2 elementary schools. He makes 2 hockey teams with boys born in 1972 (Darius Kasparaitis ), and 1976 (Danius Zubris1978 joined because his older brother was on the team).

In 1987 Aleksey was selected to attend a prestigious professional coaching school in Moscow lead by Anatolii Tarasov - The Godfather of Russian hockey.

In 1991 he came to the U.S. and had a short stint coaching with the Rangers Roger Neilson. Soon after Gerry Hart, the owner of The Rinx, asked him to take a coaching position at The Rinx in a variety of roles including coaching PAL mite kids born in 1982-83 (Mike Komisarek, Matt Anderson, Erick Nystrome, Bobby Gepford, Chris Higgins). Aleksey started to work with the Junior Islanders and met coaching legend El Arbour. In 1887-88 he coached group of kids (including Vladimir Nikiforov) all of whom made to Division I-III and even professional level. Since then he has coached and won at all levels and age groups. Most recently Aleksey won 2 Silver medals at the Division 2 U18 and U20 World Cup as head coach of team Lithuania. Below is a list of his major accomplishments.

Accomplishments:

  • Youth hockey teams won four times NY State championship
  • 1999 Silver stick champions
  • 2001 Quebec peewee tournament champions
  • 2006 Junior Bobcats all around league champions
  • 2007 3rd place in US National junior A competition
  • 2009 National junior B tournament champions.
  • 2017 Lithuania U18 silver champions
  • 2018 Lithuania U20 silver champions
